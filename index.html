FAQ

QUESTIONS
ANSWERED

Do I need a gym membership to follow these programs?

Most programs are designed for gym environments with free weights and machines. We include home-friendly modifications for every workout in the program guide.

How many days per week do I need to train?

Plans are structured for 3–6 days per week depending on the program. You can customize the schedule to fit your lifestyle — consistency matters more than frequency.

Is the nutrition guide suitable for vegetarians?

Yes! The nutrition guide includes plant-based protein sources, vegetarian and vegan meal options, and supplement recommendations for those avoiding animal products.

Can beginners use ForgeFit programs?

Absolutely. Every program has a beginner track with foundational movement patterns, lighter loading, and longer rest periods. You grow into the advanced tiers progressively.

What happens when my subscription ends?

All downloaded PDFs and materials remain yours forever. You lose access to the online portal, video library, and coach support until you renew your plan.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes — we offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee. If you follow the program and are not satisfied, we'll refund your purchase. No questions asked.
