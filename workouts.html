Training Tips

PROGRAM
GUIDELINES

Follow these principles to get the most out of every session and avoid common mistakes.

How do I warm up properly before lifting?

Spend 5–10 minutes on light cardio, then perform 2–3 warm-up sets at 40–60% of your working weight. Focus on the specific movement pattern of your first exercise.

When should I increase my training weight?

Apply the 2-rep rule: when you can complete all sets with 2 reps in reserve, increase the weight by 2.5–5 kg on the next session for that lift.

How long should my workouts be?

Most sessions should be 45–75 minutes. Beyond 90 minutes, cortisol rises and performance drops. Quality over quantity — stay focused, limit rest time, and finish strong.

Can I train the same muscle two days in a row?

Not recommended. Most muscle groups need 48–72 hours to recover and grow. Splitting your week into push, pull, and legs is the most efficient approach for most people.
